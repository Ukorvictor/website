About Veecii

Executive Profile

World-Class Sound.
Timeless Work.

Victor Ukor, professionally known as Veecii, is a world‑class music producer, songwriter, mix & master engineer, multi‑instrumentalist, and talent manager. With over a decade of professional experience and more than 500 projects, Victor has established himself as a refined architect of sound.

His work exists at the intersection of emotion and precision, where musicality meets technical mastery. From the earliest spark of inspiration to the final master, Victor delivers records that meet the highest international standards.

His creative reach spans Africa, United Kingdom, United States and the international markets — reflecting a sound that is both culturally grounded and universally resonant.
